Trend Timeline

Short-term movements and long-term shifts shaping the next seasons.

Digital Couture (2026)

Augmented fittings and bespoke digital garments create new avenues for luxury ownership and storytelling.

Sustainable Silhouettes (2027)

Regenerative fibers and repair-first design become standard language across ateliers and high-street alike.

Heritage Revival (2028)

Regional crafts are reinterpreted with contemporary tailoring — a renewed respect for provenance and makers.

Quiet Luxury (Ongoing)

Understated finishes, elevated basics and longevity over flash define a quieter, more considered wardrobe.
